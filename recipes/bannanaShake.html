Bannana Shake

Description:

This is a recipe to make a bannana shake. You will need a blender, a cup, and the following ingredients:

Ingredients:

  • Bannana
  • Milk
  • Eggs(this choice is optional and can be left out)
  • Honey(or your choice of sweetener)
  • Choice of additional fat like creamer or mct oil(or other creamer of preference)
  • Whipped Cream
  • Cinnamon

Steps:

  1. Grab your bannana and toss it in the blender.
  2. Add 8 ounces of milk or to your preference of texture.
  3. Add an ounce of creamer or whatever your preference is.
  4. Add the raw egg(s) you choose (this step is optional)
  5. Add 2 tablesppons of choice of sweetner or adjust to taste.
  6. Blend all the ingredients together until mixed to desired texture.
  7. Once finish, pour into your cup add whipped cream to top, sprinkle cinnamon and enjoy.

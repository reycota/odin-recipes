Scrambled Eggs Recipe

This recipe consists of a few whole eggs, your favorite seasonings, a frying pan, and the oil/fat of your choice.

Ingredients

  • Whole eggs
  • butter/oil of choice
  • seasonings of choice
  • frying pan

Steps

  1. Heat up the frying pan
  2. Once heated, add the fat of choice
  3. Crack the eggs into the pan
  4. Sprinkle in seasonings of choice
  5. Stir eggs until desired consistency
  6. Remove from frying pan and plate to eat

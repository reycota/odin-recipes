milk kefir

Description

This is a fermented dairy beverage with many health benefits, that taste great in many forms, like a smoothie and is easy to make.

Ingredients

  • milk of choice
  • kefir grains
  • mason jar
  • cheese cloth/coffe filter (optional)
  • stainless steel mesh strainer and bowl

Steps

  1. sanitize mason jar and lid
  2. put 1 tsp kefir grains into mason jar
  3. select milk of choice, and pour into mason jar with grains, about 2 ups per tsp is the ratio
  4. cover mason jar with lid or cheesecloth/coffee filter
  5. if kefir grains are already actiated, let sit for 24 hrs for fermentation. If not activated, follow manufacturer instructions for activation, then proceed with regular fermentation once grains are active.
  6. once fermenetation is complete, filter out into a meauring cup and strainer, separting the grains from the fermented milk.
  7. pour kefir into another mason jar, then you can put the kefir grains back in the mason jar, and make more kefir again.

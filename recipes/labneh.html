Labneh Recipe

Description

This is a very simple recipe, but it is amazing. It is like a cheese/yogurt product.

Ingredients

  • yogurt of choice, can be store bought or home made, preferably start with a thick yogurt like greek.
  • salt
  • cheese cloth/towel
  • big bowl
  • mesh strainer

Steps

  1. pour about 1 tsp of salt into yogurt of choice, and mix thoroughly
  2. once mixed in, prepare the bowl with the mesh strainer over it, and place a large cheescloth in the strainer
  3. pour your yogurt into the cheesecloth that is on your mesh strainer
  4. once all yogurt is poured in the cheesecloth, grab the cheescloth and lift up the yogurt to tighten the cloth around the yogurt
  5. ensure the cheescloth is covering all of the yogurt and is now more secure
  6. place back into the mesh strainer, and leave in fridge overnight to 24hrs
  7. once time has passed, there will be a lot of whey liquid at the bottom of the bowl, and the labneh is inside the cheesecloth
  8. take the labneh out, and plate as desired, can be garnished many ways, plate and enjoy as a spread, dip, or like thicker yogurt. can be used with sweet items like jam, or savoury like olive oil with spices and seasonings. very versatile

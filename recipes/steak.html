Steak

Description:

This is a recipe for steak to be done at the exact doneness of your preference. You will need a sous vide circulator, a pan to sear the steak or a grill, a plate for the ingredients and finished product, some butter, a pot to circulate the water , a plastic bag, and the following ingredients:

Ingredients:

  • choice of steak
  • salt and pepper (or your choice of seasoning)
  • choice of butter

Steps:

  1. Fill your empty pot with water and circulate to desired temperature using sous vide circulator.
  2. Season your steak, and place in your plastic bag.
  3. Once steaks are in bag, submerge bag under water to get all air out of it, and clip it to the side of pan
  4. Sous vide steak at desired temp for time according to sous vide machine.
  5. Once meat is cooked, remove the meat from the water bath and take out of bag.
  6. Pat the steaks dry, and begin heating your pan or grill.
  7. Once pan or grill is heated, coat the pan or grill with desired oil/fat.
  8. On high heat, sear your steak in the pan or the grill for 1-2 minutes total for both sides, so as to not overcook the steak.
  9. Once the steak has reached desired sear on both sides, immediately remove from heat, place on plage, and let rest for a few minutes.
  10. The steak should be ready to eat.
